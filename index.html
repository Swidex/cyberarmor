CyberArmor

About

CyberArmor is a smart modular shirt that allows for customization through modules connected to the shirt. The modules are controlled and connected to a smart device such as a phone. CyberArmor works through connection with Carbon Nano Tubes, a new technology made from layering graphene on each other.

CyberArmor aims to lead the revolution of wearable technology. CyberArmor aims to include every article of clothing to have modules included. CyberArmor will revolutionize the market of wearable technology.


Features


Modular


The CyberArmor clothing line included modular ports that allow for the customer to choose their selection of modules. Whether you want health, gaming, or business modules, the customer chooses!

Comfortable


By utilizing graphene, we can allow for free and comfortable movement. Graphene is a thin, flexible, and durable material that will be used for the electronics within and outside the CyberArmor.

Convenient


In this modern age, everyone must carry a phone around. By utilizing the phone as a hub, we can expand the usability of phones by having several useful modules.
